我目前就读于武汉大学新闻传播学院（智能传播方向）硕士（2024–2027），师从董庆兴教授。我的研究兴趣聚焦于复杂适应系统、群体智能以及多智能体学习。我特别关注一个核心问题：智能体之间简单的局部交互如何产生复杂的集体行为与涌现智能。

I am a master's student in the Intelligent Communication track at the School of Journalism and Communication, Wuhan University (2024–2027), advised by Prof. Qingxing Dong. My research interests lie in complex adaptive systems, collective intelligence, and multi-agent learning. I am driven by a central question: how do simple local interactions among agents give rise to complex collective behavior and emergent intelligence?

我对协调、合作、竞争、信息传播以及适应性行为等现象十分感兴趣。这些现象广泛存在于社会系统、生物群体以及人工智能系统之中。吸引我的是这样一种观点：复杂系统层面的智能往往并非来自中心化控制，而是源于大量个体之间持续的交互与学习过程。

I am deeply interested in phenomena such as coordination, cooperation, competition, information diffusion, and adaptive behavior — phenomena that permeate social systems, biological collectives, and artificial intelligence systems alike. What fascinates me is the idea that system-level intelligence often arises not from centralized control, but from sustained local interactions and learning among a large number of individuals.

在研究方法上，我主要使用网络科学、机器学习、智能体建模（Agent-Based Modeling）与大规模仿真。近年来，我开始将研究兴趣扩展到多智能体强化学习（MARL），希望利用学习型智能体研究复杂系统中的协同、适应与涌现现象。

Methodologically, I draw on network science, machine learning, agent-based modeling (ABM), and large-scale simulation. More recently, I have extended my interests to multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL), aiming to use learning agents to study coordination, adaptation, and emergence in complex systems.

我的长期目标是构建连接智能体学习机制与宏观系统行为的统一计算框架，从而理解群体智能、协作决策以及复杂适应系统中的涌现规律。我尤其希望探索学习、通信与交互如何促进集体智能的形成，以及这些机制如何被用于构建更强大的多智能体系统。

My long-term goal is to develop a unified computational framework that bridges agent-level learning mechanisms with macro-level system behavior, thereby deepening our understanding of collective intelligence, collaborative decision-making, and emergent regularities in complex adaptive systems. I am particularly eager to explore how learning, communication, and interaction facilitate the formation of collective intelligence, and how these mechanisms can be harnessed to build more capable multi-agent systems.

发表论文Publications

  • How Misinformation Diffuses on Online Social Networks: Radical Opinions, Adaptive Relationship and Algorithmic Intervention
    Mengyi Zhang, Qingxing Dong, Xiaozhen Wu
    IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems (SCI Q1), 2025
    摘要Abstract

    构建了一个动态模型，刻画算法干预下用户错误信息信念与社会关系的协同演化过程。引入同化节点与排斥节点，分析其在观点演化中的作用。整合五种推荐算法（基于内容、基于用户、基于关系、基于热度及定时推送）模拟信息分发机制。在 Python 中进行重复实验，分析推荐比例、算法类型、节点类型和重连规则对观点极化与调和的影响。

    Designed a dynamic model to capture the co-evolution of users' misinformation beliefs and social relationships under algorithmic interventions. Introduced assimilating and repelling nodes to analyze their roles in opinion evolution. Integrated five recommendation algorithms (content-based, user-based, relationship-based, popularity-based, and scheduled) to simulate information distribution mechanisms. Conducted repeated experiments in Python to analyze recommendation ratios, algorithms, node types, and rewiring rules on opinion polarization and moderation.

  • EMIF: Evidence-aware Multi-source Information Fusion Network for Explainable Fake News Detection
    Qingxing Dong, Mengyi Zhang, Shiyuan Wu, Xiaozhen Wu
    arXiv:2407.01213, 2024
    摘要Abstract

    提出一种证据感知的多源信息融合网络（EMIF），用于可解释的虚假新闻检测，联合利用用户评论与相关新闻文章。设计了协同注意力网络以捕获评论与新闻之间的语义冲突，设计了分歧选择模块以识别与原始新闻差异最大的 Top-K 相关文章，并提出不一致性损失函数以增强跨源证据一致性。在 FibVID 数据集上展示了强大的鲁棒性，即使在单一信息源不足的情况下也能保持良好性能。

    Proposed an Evidence-aware Multi-source Information Fusion (EMIF) network for explainable fake news detection that jointly leverages user comments and related news articles. Designed a co-attention network to capture semantic conflicts between comments and news, a divergence selection module to identify top-K divergent related articles, and an inconsistency loss to strengthen cross-source evidence consistency. Demonstrated strong robustness on the FibVID dataset even when one information source is insufficient.
